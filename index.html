About

I was born and raised in Ankara, Turkey. Since I was a child, I always had an interest in design and art. Painting, making clay sculptures and sketching has been a pastime for me. I finished my high school degree with IB diploma. I got into Architecture in Bilkent University. Towards the end of my architectural degree, I developed an interest in graphic design. As I graduated, I got into Hacettepe University’s masters degree of Graphic Design and learned about typography, color theory, printing, photography retouching, logo design, animation and motion graphics. I have increased my software skills by learning about 3D softwares. I also developed a deeper knowledge on Adobe Creative Suite software. I have written my thesis on the topic of “The Importance of Data Visualization in Urban Context and a Project on Environmental Problems in Ankara”. While making research for the thesis, I developed an interest in creative coding and data visualisation.

Currently I am enrolled in MSc Creative Computing in CCI, where I am expanding my skills and knowledge about computational art and design. My work involves coding, artificial intelligence, and creative softwares such as Touchdesigner and Unity, in order to create interactive and immersive art that can respond to complex ideas.
